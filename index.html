About Me

I am an archivist because I believe in preserving the voices of diverse communities to tell fuller stories about the past and present. I am also dedicated to a feminist and collaborative approach to primary source literacy and library instruction. My academic interests include community-based archives, social movement history, and gender and sexuality studies.
